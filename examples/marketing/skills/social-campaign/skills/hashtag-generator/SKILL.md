# Hashtag Generator

Generate a ranked list of hashtags for a social post based on topic and platform trends.

## Inputs

- `topic` — post subject
- `max` — maximum number of hashtags to return (default: 10)

## Outputs

- Ranked hashtag list with estimated reach score
